Darkness Surrounded 🎃

A horror survival game where the only thing scarier than the monsters...

A horror survival game built in Unity (C#) — where I designed the enemy AI behaviour, built the flashlight battery system that serves as your only lifeline,

and crafted level layouts where darkness isn't just aesthetic, it's a gameplay mechanic.

The monsters are scary. The battery percentage is scarier.

Under the Hood 🔧

  • Engine & Language: Unity · C#
  • Enemy AI: Scripted enemy patrol, detection, and chase behaviour using Unity's NavMesh and C# state logic
  • Level Design: Designed level layouts where darkness is a gameplay mechanic, not just atmosphere
  • Player Controller: Scripted first-person movement with stealth and noise detection hooks for enemy awareness
