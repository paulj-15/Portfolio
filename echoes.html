Echoes of the Flood 🌊

A timeline-hopping adventure where you outsmart a flood...

A timeline-hopping narrative game built in Unity (C#) — where I integrated Whisper AI to give the game a buddy that actually remembers what you say,

designed branching dialogue systems, and built the puzzle logic that lets you bend time to outsmart a flood.

Basically: Doctor Who, but water everywhere, and the AI has receipts.

Under the Hood 🔧

  • Engine & Language: Unreal Engine 5 · Blueprints
  • AI Integration: Integrated Whisper AI for real-time voice recognition — the AI buddy listens, processes, and actually remembers what you say
  • Narrative Systems: Designed and implemented branching dialogue logic that responds to player input and memory state
  • Puzzle Design: Built time-manipulation puzzle mechanics with Blueprint logic driving cause-and-effect across timeline states
  • Dialogue Memory System: Developed a persistent memory system that stores and references prior player conversations mid-game
  • Timeline Mechanics: Scripted the core loop that lets players shift between time states to alter world outcomes
