Hell's Shroud 🩸

A first person horror adventure built with Unreal Engine 5...

A first-person horror experience developed in Unreal Engine 5 at Jokar Studios — where I designed the level layouts,

scripted gameplay systems in Blueprints, and crafted the atmospheric lighting that makes every corridor feel like a decision you'll immediately regret.

Your doctor would not approve.

My contribution: all of the above. Right from Narrative Design to Game/Narrative Mechanics and Aesthetics to Prototyping and Experiental Mechanics.

Under the Hood 🔧

  • Engine & Language: Unreal Engine 5 · Blueprints · Horror Engine
  • Project Type: Professional project developed at Jokar Studios
  • Level Design: Designed full level layouts with intentional spatial flow — every corridor built to disorient, every room built to unsettle
  • Blueprint Systems: Scripted core gameplay mechanics and interactive elements using UE5 Blueprints
  • Atmospheric Lighting: Built lighting setups using UE5's Lumen system to create tension through visibility and shadow
  • Horror Engine Integration: Leveraged the Horror Engine framework to accelerate and enhance scare mechanics and environmental triggers
  • Environmental Storytelling: Designed level geometry and prop placement to tell the narrative without dialogue
