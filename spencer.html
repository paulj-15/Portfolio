Spencer ❄️

A cozy but perilous snowy adventure.

A cozy survival adventure built in Unity (C#) — featuring exploration mechanics, resource collection systems,

and a temperature survival loop that punishes you gently for forgetting your coat.

Warm gameplay. Cold consequences. Hot cocoa not included.

Under the Hood 🔧

  • Engine & Language: Unreal Engine 5 · Blueprints
  • Survival Loop: Designed a temperature and resource survival system — stay warm, stay alive, run out of either and the snow wins
  • Resource Collection: Built item pickup, inventory tracking, and resource management logic using Blueprints
  • Exploration Design: Designed the open snowy environment with points of interest, risk zones, and reward placement
  • Environmental Mechanics: Implemented temperature decay system tied to proximity to heat sources and time exposed to cold
  • UI & Feedback: Built in-game HUD displaying survival stats — temperature, resources, and player status
